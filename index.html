FAQ

What is a Hackathon

A hackathon is a 24 hour coding contest where participants split up into teams of up to 4 people to build something cool.

Who can attend?

Any student who is 18+. If you have graduated within the past 12 months we will consider you a student for the event also.

How much does it cost?

HackNapier is completely free! Tickets however will be on a first-come first-served basis, and space is limited.

What do I need to bring?

A laptop and power supply are the essentials. Aside from that, you may want a phone charger, headphones. On the non-tech side, consider an extra set of clothes, toiletries and the like.

Does my team have to all be from the same uni?

Not at all! Hackathons are a good way to be able to meet hackers from all over the country, and as such you can feel free to team up with people from other unis!

Can I work on an existing project?

Sorry, but not if you want to be eligible for any of the prizes. You can brainstorm, research etc but do not start building until the start of hacking.

Can I volunteer?

Drop us an email at hi@hacknapier.uk and tell us a little about yourself and we'll take it from there!

Do I need to know how to code?

Absolutely not! Hackathons are an amazing learning experience, and you will be able to learn loads from other hackers in your team over the course of the event.
We'll also have mentors at the event who can help you out with learning skills during the hack.

Can I sponsor HackNapier?

We'd really appreciate anyone who would like to help make HackNapier possible.
Hackathons are awesome for those that hack but also a really great platform to display who you are and what you do to a bunch of students that are clearly interested in technology and enjopy building cool projects. Fire us an email at hi@hacknapier.uk and we'll work something out!
